# 💊 PharmaVault IMS – Smart Pharmaceutical Inventory Management System

🚀 **PharmaVault IMS** is a powerful and intelligent Inventory Management System designed specifically for the **pharmaceutical domain**. It streamlines the management of medicines across **companies, warehouses, and vendors** with efficiency, accuracy, and scalability.

---

## 🧠 Overview

Managing pharmaceutical inventory is complex — from stock tracking to vendor coordination and warehouse logistics.

💡 **PharmaVault IMS solves this by providing:**

* Centralized medicine inventory management
* Seamless coordination between stakeholders
* Real-time stock tracking and updates
* Efficient workflow automation

---

## ⚙️ Key Features

✨ **Inventory Management**

* Track medicine stock in real-time
* Manage batch details, expiry dates, and quantities

🏢 **Multi-Entity Handling**

* Supports multiple companies
* Warehouse-to-warehouse transfers
* Vendor integration & management

📦 **Stock Movement Tracking**

* Monitor incoming & outgoing stock
* Maintain transparent transaction logs

📊 **User-Friendly Interface**

* Clean dashboards
* Easy navigation across modules

🔐 **Data Integrity**

* Reliable and structured data handling
* Reduced chances of manual errors
